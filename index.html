UB40

The English reggae and pop band, formed in December 1978 in Birmingham, England. With more than 50 singles in the UK Singles Chart and having sold over 70 million records worldwide UB40 is one of the most internationally recogonised bands.

The name "UB40" was selected in reference to a form issued to people claiming unemployment benefits from the UK government's Department of Employment. The designation UB40 stood for Unemployment Benefit, Form 40.

Terence Wilson

Terence Wilson (born 24 June 1957 in Birmingham), better known by his stage name Astro, is a British musician, rapper, and toaster, who was part of UB40 from 1979 until he left the group in November 2013.
